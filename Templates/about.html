Breast Cancer Prediction Using Deep Learning

Breast cancer is one of the most prevalent diseases affecting women worldwide and remains a leading cause of death. Traditional detection methods often require extensive medical testing and expert evaluation, which can delay diagnosis and treatment.

Our project leverages advanced deep learning models to enable faster and more accurate prediction of breast cancer from medical images. By applying effective feature selection techniques, we improve model accuracy, helping patients receive early treatment.

At our platform, we are committed to raising awareness and promoting innovation in healthcare. We believe that technology can play a vital role in making healthcare more accessible and effective.

Our Focus:

  • Promoting early detection through AI-driven predictions.
  • Empowering doctors with advanced diagnostic tools.
  • Raising awareness about breast cancer prevention.
  • Encouraging research and innovation in healthcare AI.
